In 2019 , shoppers aged between 45 and 64 accounted for the majority of pet store revenue with a 43.5 percent share , and therefore made up the largest target market in the United States . Those aged between 25 and 44 made up the second largest market , providing 33.1 percent of pet store revenue in the same year . Continued importance of brick-and-mortar stores in the pet market Ninety-three percent of cat owners and 88 percent of dog owners continue to purchase pet food in physical storesin the U.S .
